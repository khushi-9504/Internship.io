About Udemy

Udemy is a platform that allows instructors to host online courses on their preferred topics. Instructors can upload videos and other educational material. Courses are offered in various categories, including business, academics, health and fitness, and music. Most classes are in practical subjects such as AWS and Azure training, Excel software or using an iPhone camera.
